Common Phishing Indicators:

⚠️ Misspelled Domains

e.g., "gg00gle.com" instead of "google.com"

⚠️ Suspicious Extensions

URLs with IP addresses or unusual TLDs

⚠️ Secure Connection

Check for HTTPS and valid SSL certificate

⚠️ Shortened URLs

Links like bit.ly that hide actual destination

⚠️ Urgency Tactics

Emails claiming immediate action required

⚠️ Suspicious Emails

Requests for password or personal info

🛡️ How to Avoid Phishing:

  • Never click links in unsolicited emails - go directly to official website
  • Check sender's email address carefully
  • Look for HTTPS and padlock icon in address bar
  • Hover over links to see actual URL before clicking
  • Be suspicious of urgent requests or threats
  • Contact company directly if you're unsure about a link
  • Legitimate companies never ask for passwords via email

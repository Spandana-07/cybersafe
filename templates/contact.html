Frequently Asked Questions

Is my password saved when I use the Password Checker?

No, your passwords are checked locally in your browser and never sent to our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

How accurate is the Phishing URL Detector?

Our detector uses multiple heuristics to identify suspicious URLs. However, for absolute confirmation, check the official website directly or contact the organization.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, our basic security tools are free and available to everyone. Registration is optional for saving your security assessments.

How often is the security information updated?

We update our security tips and threat information regularly to reflect the latest cyber threats and best practices.

Can I use CyberSafe for my organization?

Yes! We offer enterprise solutions for organizations. Please contact us at info@cybersafe.com for more details.

What browsers do you support?

CyberSafe works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Please keep your browser updated.
